"You are here → AGI" — hero concepts

Three takes on the famous curve, tuned for an "are we AGI yet? → No" site.

Concept A

The Goalposts

The "AGI" sign keeps rolling away. Every past "almost there" is still bunched down in the flats.

Vibe: dry, skeptical, on-brand for "No."  ·  Tagline: "AGI is always five years away."

Concept B

Hype vs. Reality

Two lines: the timeline on your feed (rocketing to AGI) and what the benchmarks actually show (a polite crawl).

Vibe: playful, sharable.  ·  Tagline: "We're the yellow line. We've always been the yellow line."

Concept C

Not to scale

Same dot, two x-axes. It feels like AGI is right there — until you read the axis.

Vibe: the smart-aleck data-viz joke.  ·  Tagline: "Feels close. Check the axis labels."
